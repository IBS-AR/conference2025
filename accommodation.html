The conference venue, National Film and Sound Archive of Australia (NFSA), is within walking distance to CBD, so any CBD hotel is generally suitable.

Discounts for Delegates

  • Novotel Canberra (15% off Best Available Rate, discount is subject to availability)
  • Peppers Gallery Hotel Canberra (No pre-payment required, cancel up to 1 day prior to arrival date) Enter the promo code EVENTSPECIAL25
  • Midnight Hotel enter the code KF7 at the Corp/Promo Code area under Special Rates
